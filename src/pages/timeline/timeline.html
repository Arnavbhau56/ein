Seed Stars Journey: July to October 2025

Your path from application to funding in 6 structured phases

23rd July - 21st Aug

Startup Registrations : Inviting registrations from startups, primarily those in the seed to early growth stages.

25th Aug - 31st Aug

Initial Screening : Top startups reviewed by VC analysts for shortlisting.

3rd Sep - 14th Sep

First Pitching : Shortlisted startups pitch directly to VCs for cohort selection.

16th Sep - 12th Oct

Cohort & Mentoring : Selected startups undergo structured mentoring with VC partners.

15th Oct - 26th Oct

Final Pitching : Finalists pitch offline at IIT Bombay to explore investment opportunities.
