About E-Cell
The Entrepreneurship Cell of IIT Bombay is Asia’s largest student-run, non-profit organization dedicated to fostering entrepreneurship. With a legacy spanning 28 years, it has positively impacted millions across India and beyond.
IEntO
E-Cell IIT Bombay’s Campus Ambassador Program is designed to spark the entrepreneurial spirit in students across India. This internship gives you hands-on experience in the startup world while helping you gain practical skills, grow your network, and become a leader on your campus.
