IEntO
The International Entrepreneurship Olympiad (IEntO) is India's first-ever offline competition aimed at fostering entrepreneurship among school students. Organized by E-Cell IIT Bombay, IEntO offers a structured platform to nurture leadership and business acumen in young students. The Olympiad provides a competitive environment where students from classes 6 to 12 can showcase their entrepreneurial skills and collaborate with like-minded peers from across the country.
Our Vision
Our vision is to develop and nurture the entrepreneurial spirit in young minds by providing a platform that allows them to explore, innovate, and achieve their dreams in the field of entrepreneurship. Through this Olympiad, we aim to position entrepreneurship as a viable and rewarding career option by integrating it with academic excellence, empowering the next generation of leaders to shape a brighter future.
